Harry James Potter is the main character of the Harry Potter story. He is a young wizard and a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
Hermione Granger is a student at Hogwarts and one of Harry Potter's close friends.
Ron Weasley is a student at Hogwarts and one of Harry Potter's close friends.
Professor McGonagall is a teacher at Hogwarts.
Hagrid is the gamekeeper at Hogwarts and introduces the first-year students to the castle.
